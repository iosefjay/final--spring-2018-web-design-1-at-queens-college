I'm a fan of the simple things. I enjoy stuff when when you think about it and get real deep into it, it's not that complicated. A simple design can have so big an effect. And thats what i find interesting, that you can do so little and yet leave such a big impact. Because we all like to do the same things for less, in fact thats what technology is for isn't it haha. And that is what I want to achieve with my pieces. Something that is simple yet it does so much.I would really enjoy that.
