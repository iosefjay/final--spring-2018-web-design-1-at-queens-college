My name is Denzel.A.J Williams, photography was always something I've done just for the joy of it. I never procured the idea of growing in this filed until my early 20's when I was introduced to street photographers such as Henri Cartier, Garry Winogrand and Elliott Erwitt all great photographer with amazing composition in their work. Even though, I always had a camera my passion always and forever be drawing and painting that which soon became the foundation of my career as D.Alex that which began in 2016.
