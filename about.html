About the artist

Graphic design student based in Ridgewood, Queens. Currently studying at Queens College. Interested in design with a focus on web design, interactive art, and vector art. I graduated LaGuardia Community College with an associates degree in Fine Arts. There I honed my skills in mixed media art and art history. I enjoy producing work that is thought provoking.
